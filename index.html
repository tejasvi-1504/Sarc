Engineering Roads That Last Generations

SARC Pvt Limited is a premier highway infrastructure company headquartered in Bijnor, Uttar Pradesh. With over 15 years of excellence, we have successfully delivered more than 50 highway projects across the region.

Post Completion

Always There For Your Roads

Our commitment doesn't end at project handover. Comprehensive maintenance to preserve road quality for decades.

Routine Maintenance

Scheduled inspections, pothole patching, line marking renewal, and vegetation control to keep highways in peak condition year-round.

Emergency Response
